Both parties, intending to be legally bound, agree as follows:
1. Scope of Services
1.1 Scan2Food provides a QR-based food ordering platform that allows moviegoers to order food from their seats.
1.2 The Theatre agrees to integrate Scan2Food's services and facilitate order fulfillment.
1.3 Scan2Food will provide a dashboard for theatre staff to manage and process orders.
2. Revenue Sharing & Payment Terms
2.1 Scan2Food charges a {{theatre.detail.commission}}% convenience fee on each transaction made through the platform.
2.2 The convenience fee is collected from customers at the time of placing an order.
2.3 The Theatre will receive the food order amount (excluding the convenience fee) in weekly settlements via bank transfer.
2.4 Both parties will be responsible for their applicable GST and tax liabilities.
3. Responsibilities of the Theatre
3.1 Ensure timely preparation and delivery of food orders.
3.2 Maintain food hygiene, quality standards, and pricing consistency.
3.3 Provide customer support for food-related issues and order complaints.
4. Responsibilities of Scan2Food
4.1 Maintain a stable and secure platform for ordering and payment processing.
4.2 Provide technical support to the Theatre for system-related issues.
4.3 Facilitate real-time order notifications and analytics for better service management.
5. Data Privacy & Confidentiality
5.1 Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of customer data, including payment and order information.
5.2 The Theatre shall not misuse or share customer data with third parties.
6. Liability & Indemnification
6.1 The Theatre is solely responsible for food preparation, pricing, quality, and delivery.
6.2 Scan2Food shall not be liable for food-related complaints or disputes.
6.3 Each party agrees to indemnify the other against any claims arising from a breach of this Agreement.
7. Marketing & Branding Rights
7.1 The Theatre grants Scan2Food permission to use its name and logo for marketing and promotional activities.
7.2 The Theatre agrees to display Scan2Food QR codes, branding materials, and app references at appropriate locations.
8. Term & Termination
8.1 This Agreement is valid for a period of 1 year, with an automatic renewal unless either party provides a written notice 30 days prior to expiration.
8.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' prior written notice.
8.3 In case of a breach, the affected party may terminate the Agreement immediately upon written notice.
9. Dispute Resolution
9.1 Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through negotiation and mediation.
10. Force Majeure
10.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond their control (e.g., natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.).
11. Miscellaneous
11.1 Amendments to this Agreement shall be made in writing and signed by both parties.
11.2 Both parties agree to comply with all applicable government laws and regulations.
