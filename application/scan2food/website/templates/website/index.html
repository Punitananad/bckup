About Us

At Scan2Food, we provide a cutting-edge service that enables theaters to deliver snacks and meals to customers without them leaving their seats. Our easy-to-use platform helps increase concession sales, reduce long lines, and create a premium movie-watching experience. Let your audience enjoy the film while boosting your operational efficiency.

Why Choose Scan2Food?
Boost Concession Sales

By allowing customers to order from their seats, Scan2Food encourages more frequent and impulse purchases, increasing your overall concession revenue.

Enhance Customer Experience

Make movie-going more enjoyable for your patrons by eliminating the need to stand in long lines. Scan2Food keeps your customers in their seats, ensuring they don’t miss a moment of the movie while enjoying their favorite snacks.

Streamline Operations for Your Staff

With our platform, your staff can manage orders more efficiently using tablets or mobile devices. This minimizes disruptions in the theater and ensures a smooth, hassle-free service.

How it works

Scan the QR Code

Place QR codes on each seat or table, allowing customers to easily scan and access your menu from their smartphones.

Choose from the Menu

Your customers can view your full concession menu, including snacks, beverages, and meals, right from their phones.

Simple, Secure Ordering

Customers place their orders and pay securely through the app. Orders are instantly sent to your kitchen staff for preparation.

Deliver Directly to Their Seat

Your staff delivers the food directly to the customer’s seat, ensuring a seamless, enjoyable experience without movie interruptions.

Pricing
Convenience Fee from Customer
  • 5% fee on each order, paid by the customer.
  • No upfront costs or extra fees for theatres.
  • Theatre incurs no expense.
Convenience Fee from Theatre
  • 5% fee on digital sales charged to theatre.
  • Theatre can raise menu prices to cover it.
  • No cost to the customer.
  • Extra earnings for seat-on service.
  • No subscription or upfront fees.
Annual Subscription Model
  • ₹40,000 per auditorium per year.
  • No per-order charges to customer or theatre.
  • Flat, transparent pricing model.
