About Us

At Scan2Food, we make watching movies even better by letting you order your favorite snacks and meals without leaving your seat. With our service, you won’t miss any part of the movie. Just scan the QR code, choose what you want from the menu, and get your food delivered right to you.

Why Choose Scan2Food?
Skip the Long Lines

Forget standing in long concession lines. With Scan2Food, you can order without leaving your seat, saving time and avoiding crowds.

Never Miss a Scene

No more rushing during movie breaks or waiting until intermission. With our service, you can order anytime — before or during the movie.

Easy for Theatre Staff

Theatre staff can manage orders efficiently using tablets or mobile phones, ensuring quick service without disrupting the movie experience.

How it works

Scan the QR Code

Use your phone to scan the QR code available at your seat.

Choose from the Menu

Browse a wide range of snacks, beverages, and meals.

Order with Ease

Place your order, pay securely, and sit back while your food is prepared.

Enjoy Delivery to Your Seat

No more long lines or missing the movie — your food is delivered right to your seat!!
